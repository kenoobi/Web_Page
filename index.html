    Review

    Santiago Benitez Tamayo (Medellin, October 8, 1994) known as Santiago Beta or Santiago0beta is a liveCoder, improviser, algorithmic composer and music producer from the city of Medellín / Colombia.
    Shows interest in electronic music and programming, this curiosity led him to be part of the group of researchers Algo0ritmos of the city of Medellin, which exists since the end of 2013.
    Collective that strives to bring algorithms and mathematical logic to the dance floor using the Chuck Programming software. Santiago is part of the creative ideas of Chmusick, a library of free use to facilitate livecoding and teaching of it.

    Music

    Within the group algo0ritmos, Santiago is part of multiple presentations over time. (Some important presentations):

    * Performance at the MAMM (Museum of Modern Art of Medellin 2016).
    * Performance in the first Algorave in South America (South America) for the year 2017.
    * Performance on the birthday of the Algorave movement (algofive and algosix).
    * Performance in the city of Morelia (Mexico) and CDMX in its first international festival of live coding (ICLC17).
    * Musical production selected by the Ministry of Culture of Morelia (Mexico) to give sound to its video dismissed from 2017.
    * Workshop at the First Real-Time Arts Festival Medellín 2018.

    Investigation

    It is a research / practical project where information is collected on the different rhythms and poly-rhythms that make up commercial music around the world and where listening exercises, analysis and creation of contemporary music are performed. In order to understand and bring your musical notation to its minimal expression (MIDI / binary), the main idea of ​​this workshop is to make known the essential and essential characteristics that identify the different genres that make up the EDM (electronic dance music), As the composition process, tricks in music production, mastering, analog synthesis, filters and equalization. In this way, to gather the necessary and useful elements to face the performance from the theoretical / practical point of view and achieve an optimal result in the process of improvisation when performing the livecoding. giving a more festive and commercial character to the presentations. since the group of researchers algo0ritmos has like aim like the movement algorave or the purest meaning of livecoding to bring algorithms and mathematical logic to the dance floor.
